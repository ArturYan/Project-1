Insurance Claims and Towing!

Towing Service

BlackStar Motors will provide you a towing service (if needed). Tow trucks offer a life-line for many distressed drivers dealing with accidents. We can bring you and your vehicle straight to our shop, ensuring you don't have to spend time on the roadside as you try to work out a solution.

Insurance claim Services

An unexpected car accident can leave you feeling a bit scattered. That's why it's important to brush up on post-accident procedures now, when you're good and levelheaded. Our guide can help make the moments after an accident less stressful — and the claims process a whole lot smoother. We work with all major Insurance Companies! Just give us a call to set up a free consultation on your car and help you understand how important it is to file a claim.
